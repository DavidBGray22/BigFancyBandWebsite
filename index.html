Bio

They are a Richmond,VA based dreamy soulful, r&b, rock, pop band. Big Fancy was founded on friendship and as they began playing and crafing songs together their tight-sound reflected their connection as friends first before bandmates. Their songs vary from soft, slow, confessional reveries to disco crazed dance anthems. They blend their influences of classic funk and soul with their love of modern indie-rock, dream pop, and r&b. India and Nathan's voices fuse with sweet, silky, soultry melodic contours and they dance onstage with electrifying joyful charisma while Grady, David, Jonah give the vocals a groovy launch pad to soar from. This band began in a Richmond alleyway garage with neighbors stopping by to listen, and their music and performances are destined to make them friends with audiences everywhere.
